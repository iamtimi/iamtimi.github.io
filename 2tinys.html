Project Background

My sister and I started our own small art business under the name 2tinys. Keeping the name short and sweet, plus it's easy to associate with us because neither of us are that tall! We were inspired to start this venture while seeing other artists' journey on Instagram, we started getting serious about our venture during the summer of 2023.

We pitched our idea and became a part of the University of Houston's Red Labs Summer Accelerator program where we started working on fleshing out our brand and actively sharing our art online.

Currently, all designs are drawn by us and produced in house with the help of our Cricut! We're currently looking into creating other stationery items that will require manfucturing such as washi tape, key chains, and perhaps even tote bags and other wearable items!

2tinys - Branding

Logo, colors, font

2tinys uses a custom font of my bubble handwriting as our brand's main font, it's available in filled or outlined. I wanted to have something unique for us to use on our sticker sheets or packaging. Making it into a font is cool bonus because we're able to use it in videos too!



During the accelerator program, I worked on updating our previous Instagram icon into a cleaner, useable logo while keeping a similar color pallette.
