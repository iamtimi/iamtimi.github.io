Division of Behavioral & Social Sciences Rebrand

A complete rebrand for Division 4: Window Cling, Pull-up Banners

Design: I chose the magnifying glass as the key visual piece for Division 4 to show the depth of what they are all about. Diving deep into various aspects of the human mind and human interations. The light texture in the background acts as both the network on the brain as well as to show the connectedness of it all.

Creating individual banners for each department under the Division's umbrella, I wanted to make sure the banners are cohesive when used side by side while also being able to easily differentiable. I decided to color code the departments and use images that represent each department in the magnifying glass as well as the background.

Everything is tied together in the main banner (which is also used for their office window cling!) to show that everything can be found in one place - the Division of Behavioral & Social Sciences.


In Use: Window Cling + Pull-up Banner featured on Division 4's Instagram page



The Library Marketing

Card Handouts, On Campus Digital Screens, Flyers

Card Design: The Library is slowly working on finding ways to build awareness of the different services offered to students. I've been working hand in hand with the area to create new marketing that follows their newly made color palette - colors that match the carpet unique to the Library area!

The first step were these index card style handouts. They plan on using them at tabling events as well as keeping it at the front desk in the Library so students can take one as needed.

Front & Back Card Mockup

Digital Screen Design: A huge project for the Library has been utilizing the campus digital screens to advertise the different services given by the area. As the slides are timed short intervals, text is kept to a minimum and images are used to enchance the message so the main idea can be captured quickly.

There are horizontal & vertical screens so the design was adjusted accordingly.


Flyers & Posters Designs: The above designs were also remade to poster size for later use. In addition, there is a condesed flyer version that can be used on websites digitally as well as printed handouts for students.

The Library also requested a simple digital flyer design to advertise the workshops available in the space.


In Use: A couple of slides seen on our vertical screens!
