August 2018 - May 2019


The Details

Company: Ingrem US
Audience: Company Instagram and Facebook followers, Newsletter subscribers

Work Background

Ingrem is an up and coming company that creates and sells ergonomic gaming workstations. I had the opportunity to work for their small start up in the United States called Ingrem US. While my job duties mainly consisted of updating and maintaining their website, I was also tasked with creating their marketing graphics. During my time there, I made ad designs and social media posts for all their platforms (Instagram, Facebook, and newsletters) and saw their Instagram followers grow by nearly 200 followers.
I worked in Photoshop, but occasionally used Illustrator to create illustrations.
