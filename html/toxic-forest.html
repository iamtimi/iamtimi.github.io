Game Illustrations: Toxic Forest

Interactive Game Development Course (August 2017)


The Details

My Role: Art Director, Illustrator
Audience: Judged by a panel of professionals in the gaming industry

Project Background

In this course, my team (consisting of 3 others) were tasked with making a complete game using the Unity engine. As the Art Director, I was in charge of the art direction and visuals of the game. I created all character designs and game animations, majority of the background assets, and reviewed any other assets sent in for quality before it gets put into the game.

Our game, "Toxic Forest", is a 2D platformer where you play as the main character Buster the Beaver as he goes on a journey to save his forest.


Buster's various animations: throw, bite, tail attack, and death.
