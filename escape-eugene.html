Game Illustrations: Escape Eugene

Game Art & Animation Course (August 2016)


The Details

My Role: Illustrator
Audience: Judged by a panel of professionals in the gaming industry

Project Background

This course was my introduction to game design and the Unity engine. I learned the basics of creating game assets, animation, and the importance of UX/UI design in order to create a mobile game with my small team of 2 others. I was in charge of the interface designs, maps, and all background assets. I also coded the script to have an automatic, seamlessly repeating background to make the infinite runner possible.

"Escape Eugene!" is a 2D endless runner (with small mini games) that was designed for the Android mobile system. You play as the main character, Eugene, who was sucked into his favorite video game. You must beat all 3 maps in order to escape.
