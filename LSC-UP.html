Work Background

Lone Star College-University Park is one of eight colleges within the Lone Star College System. The college recently celebrated it's 10 year anniversary this December. The department of College Relations acts as an in-house marketing hub for LSC-UP. As a graphic designer, I get to connect with various departments and divisions across campus to create designs to fulfill their needs. The requests vary from simply updating previous designs with new information to a full campaign with prints and digital marketing!

Working together with other designers, we internally proof each other's work to ensure that the marketing that goes out is accurate and follows the college's marketing and design guidlines. I also work closely with our communications and social media team make sure the campaigns are reaching their intended audience.
