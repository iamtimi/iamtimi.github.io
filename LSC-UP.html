Work Background

Lone Star College-University Park is one of eight colleges within the Lone Star College System. The college celebrated it's 10 year anniversary in December 2022. The department of College Relations acts as an in-house marketing hub for LSC-UP. As a graphic designer, I get to connect with various departments and divisions across campus to create designs to fulfill their needs. The requests vary from simply updating previous designs with new information to a full campaign with prints and digital marketing!

Working together with other designers, we internally proof each other's work to ensure that the marketing that goes out is accurate and follows the college's marketing and design guidlines. I also work closely with our communications and social media team make sure the campaigns are reaching their intended audience.

Below are a handful of designs that I have created since working at Lone Star College-University Park.


Full Campaign - Enrollment Spring 2023

Enrollment is a major period for the college and each year the advising team comes up with a theme they would like to use for the enrollment event. I had the pleasure of creating the campaign graphics for this year's circus theme.

The graphics come in pairs as there needs to be graphics for the Early Enrollment Day Event and then the repurposed generic enrollment graphics that will be used for the remainder of the semester.

As the theme is yearly, the graphic needs to be easily editable for next semesters events and enrollment dates as well.

Misc. Flyers

Our Library is debuting a new project called the "Learn More Series" inspired by Boston University's series of the same name. Each year, they are going to focus on a big picture theme to host events, discuss, and bring awareness to. The topic for the series' debut is "Censored Lives". The planned events so far will take you through the stories of the LGBTQ+ community to a heated discussion of the censorship of books in our country.

I worked together with our librarians to come up with a logo and tag line for the series. They key to the design was to keep it simple as the logo will be used for all future "Learn More Series" event marketing so it needs to be distingushable but not distracting.

I believe the light bulb works best as the visual representation of the series as it shines a light on themes and topics that tend to be kept in the dark. The series is meant to bring together different perspectives not to persuade the listener, but to give them information so they can then go and form their own informative opinions.

The tag line is used to reiterate all the above in a concise and sweet manner: "Illuminating Perspectives. Inspiring Discussions."


As all our designs have to include the college logo as well, I knew the "Learn More Series" logo must not clash with it or take up any more space than necessary. These 2 versions (horizontal & square) are the ones the team liked the most.
